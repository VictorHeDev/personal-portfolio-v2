Emergency Medical Worker
To Software Engineer.

After graduating from the University at Buffalo (SUNY) in 2019 with a degree in Biomedical Sciences, I worked for several years in the healthcare industry as an EMT, medical assistant, and I volunteered my time as a firefighter. I am passionate about helping others and giving back to my community.

My experiences as a healthcare worker during the height of COVID, combined with my interest in the EPIC inter-facility healthcare software, and my desire to impact the lives of more people drove me change careers.

From a young age, I have always enjoyed learning about and using technology. I enjoy keeping up with the news on the latest innovations, discussing it with others, and tinkering with devices. I am a problem solver who loves flexing his creative muscles. Please feel free to reach out if you would like to talk some more or work on a project together!
